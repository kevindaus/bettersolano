Complaints Against Violations of Public Market Code

Filing complaints against violations of sanitation, hygiene, and other market rules and regulations

Service Description

  • Any individual or stallholder may file a formal complaint against violations of the Public Market Code
  • Complaints may include sanitation issues, illegal vending, and other violations
StepActivityFeeDurationPersonnel
1 Secure and fill out Public Market complaint form and submit to the Market Supervisor Free 10 minutes Market Supervisor

Issuance of Community Tax Certificate (Cedula)

As deputized by the Municipal Treasurer

Requirements

  • Fill out CTC application slip
  • Valid ID
  • Previous year's CTC (if available)
StepActivityFeeDurationPersonnel
1 Fill out CTC application slip — 2 minutes Market Supervisor
2 Pay CTC fee ₱5.00+ 2 minutes Market Supervisor
TypeBasic FeeAdditional
Individual (Basic)₱5.00+ ₱1.00 per ₱1,000 income
Corporation/Business₱500.00+ additional based on gross receipts

Processing Information

  • Total processing time: 5 minutes
  • CTC is valid for one calendar year
  • Please accomplish Client Feedback Form and drop at the designated suggestion box
