RSBSA Registration (Farm Registration)

Registry System for Basic Sectors in Agriculture - Official farmer registration for government programs

Requirements for New Registration

  • Valid ID (any government-issued ID)
  • Barangay Certification
  • Land Title / Tax Declaration / Lease Contract
  • 1x1 or 2x2 ID Photo
  • Sketch of Farm Location

Requirements for Update/Renewal

  • Old RSBSA Form / Reference Number
  • Valid ID
  • Updated Land Documents (if changed)
  • Recent 1x1 or 2x2 Photo

Fee

Free of charge for all farmers

No Payment Required

Processing Time

Same day registration upon complete submission

15-30 Minutes

Benefits

Access to government subsidies, insurance, and agricultural programs
