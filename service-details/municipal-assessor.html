About Property Assessment

Property assessment determines the fair market value and assessed value of real properties (land, buildings, machinery) for real property tax computation. This is required for new constructions, improvements, and property transactions.

1

Submit Application

File application at the Assessor's Office with required documents including proof of ownership.

2

Property Inspection

Assessor's staff will conduct on-site inspection and measurement of the property.

3

Valuation & Assessment

Property is appraised based on Schedule of Fair Market Values and assessment levels.

4

Issuance of Tax Declaration

New Tax Declaration is issued reflecting the assessed value of the property.

Required Documents

Transfer Certificate of Title
Tax Declaration (if existing)
Building Permit (for new)
Valid ID of Owner

Tax Declaration Services

Tax Declaration is an official document showing the assessed value of a property. It is used as basis for computing real property taxes and serves as proof of property ownership for tax purposes.

New Tax Declaration

For newly constructed buildings, improvements, or newly discovered properties.

Revision/Update

For changes in property details, corrections, or general revision updates.

Duplicate Copy

Replacement for lost, damaged, or additional copies of tax declaration.

Transfer of Property Ownership

When property changes ownership through sale, donation, inheritance, or other means, the tax declaration must be transferred to the new owner's name.

1

Secure Tax Clearance

Obtain tax clearance from Treasurer's Office showing no unpaid real property taxes.

2

Pay Transfer Tax

Pay the required transfer tax at the Treasurer's Office (0.5% of selling price or fair market value).

3

Submit Documents

File application with complete documents at the Assessor's Office.

4

Claim New Tax Declaration

New Tax Declaration under the new owner's name will be issued.

Required Documents

Deed of Sale/Donation
Transfer Certificate of Title
Tax Clearance
Transfer Tax Receipt
CAR/eCAR from BIR
Valid IDs (Buyer/Seller)
