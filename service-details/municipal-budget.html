Preparation, Review and Endorsement of Barangay Budget

The Municipal Budget Office assists barangays in the preparation of their Annual Budget. It conducts training whenever necessary and ensures compliance with statutory, contractual obligations and budgetary requirements prior to the review and approval of the Sangguniang Bayan.

Important Note

  • Within ten days (10) from the approval of the Barangay Budget, copies of their Annual Budget shall be furnished to the Sangguniang Bayan for review through the Municipal Budget Office.
Step Activity Documents Required Duration Personnel
1 Submit Barangay Budget for review and evaluation
  • Annual Barangay Budget
  • Transmittal letter of the Barangay Secretary
  • Budget Message of the Punong Barangay
  • The Authorized Expenditure Program for the Budget Year or Appropriation Ordinance
  • Budget of Expenditures and Sources of Financing
  • Barangay Development Plan and Barangay Annual Investment Plan

Preparation and Implementation of Special Education Fund Budget

The Municipal Budget Office assists the different schools in the preparation of SEF Annual Budget. It assists the Municipal School Board in the conduct of budget hearings and deliberations of budget proposals. It ensures compliance with statutory, contractual obligations and budgetary requirements prior to the review and approval by the Municipal School Board (MSB).

1 Submit SEF Budget for review and evaluation
